Mushroom Masala

Ingredients

  • 250g mushrooms
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Preparation

  1. Heat oil and add onions. Fry till golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook 1 minute.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices. Cook till soft.
  4. Add mushrooms and cook for 5–7 minutes.
  5. Add garam masala and simmer 2 minutes.
